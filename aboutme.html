Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

EC-Council's Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification is a globally recognized credential that validates the skills and knowledge of individuals in identifying and addressing vulnerabilities in computer systems and networks. CEH-certified professionals are known as ethical hackers or penetration testers. They use their expertise to simulate cyberattacks in controlled environments to discover and fix security weaknesses before malicious hackers can exploit them.

Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator (CHFI)

EC-Council offers the CHFI certification, which stands for Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator. This program imparts skills in investigating computer security incidents and digital forensics. CHFI-certified individuals are usually experts in cybersecurity, digital forensics, law enforcement, or IT security, capable of managing cybercrime investigations and security incidents. Keep in mind that this information is accurate as of January 2022, and there may have been developments after that date.
